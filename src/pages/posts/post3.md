# Overview

It's been a minute since I used my website and I feel bad for neglecting it for such a long time even though it took me a lot to build it. I don't know if it's just me but sometimes I wanna do so many projects, that I start preparing them
and then I realize after some time that I want to move to something else: my attention span - or in this case, "projects span" - diminish every single day. I would like to pursue a long-time projects, without my brain wanting it to switch 
for *some* reason. But anyway, It's february and I would like to wrap my 2025 in a few words so that I can start
talking about what I have been thinking in these days (some of these thoughts may be a hot take, so I would 
suggest you to take a look if you are interested) and some of my projects I have been working on.
In 2025 I have: 

  - Started learning Rust with the intentions of making my main language.
  - ...as a consequence started reading the rust-book and made some simple projects like 
  [wordle_tui](https://github.com/MagiHotline/word_tui) where you can play the famous 
  word-guessing game in your terminal.
